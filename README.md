## ✨ Premium Features

* **🌗 Dual Language Story Mode:** Choose between **English Aesthetic** 🤍 or **Banglish Emotional** 🖤 stories.
* **💎 Glassmorphism UI:** Modern frosted glass effect with a beautiful animated glass-shine sweeping across the card.
* **🌊 Animated Background:** A soothing, moving pastel gradient background featuring floating hearts and stars.
* **💬 Interactive Chat Experience:** Features live typing indicators (`💬...`) and typing sound effects before messages appear.
* **⏳ Live 3D Countdown:** A dramatic 5-second pop-up countdown synced with ticking audio.
* **🎊 Grand Finale:** A beautiful climax featuring realistic confetti blasts and a falling birthday emoji rain.
* **🎵 Immersive Audio:** Background music, tap sounds, tick-tock countdowns, and typing effects synced perfectly with the animations.
* **🚀 Direct Social Sharing:** 1-click share buttons for WhatsApp, Facebook, Telegram, and Instagram.
* **📸 Download Card:** Users can easily download their final customized birthday card as a high-quality PNG image.

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Enter Details:** Type the Name and Relationship of the birthday person.
2. **Select Language:** Choose your preferred story language (English or Banglish).
3. **Generate Magic:** Click "Create Magic ✨" to generate a unique, shareable link.
4. **Share:** Copy the link or use the direct social media buttons to send it directly to the birthday person!
